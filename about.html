About Me

In addition to taxes, she also does bookkeeping and leads Tax School in the off season for new preparers. She has been the leading tax preparer for 4 years with the most Tax Returns filed in a season between the franchisee's four locations. Although, Ruth loves helping and educating people about their Taxes she is ready for a career change. After spending so much time on the computer Ruth has grown to love it and wants to learn more about it. Therefore she is now persuing a career change in Software Enginner.
